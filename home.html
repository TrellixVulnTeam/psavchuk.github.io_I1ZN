Technology

This website hopes to explore how the job industry (the technology) is affected by the social issue of lookism, as well as how specific parts of hiring are affected or affect it, such as LinkedIn as well as the laws surrounding the process.

Lookism is the concept where our society is driven on how an individual looks, and as beauty standards rapidly change and shift, people are able to succeed more easily based on their appearance.
