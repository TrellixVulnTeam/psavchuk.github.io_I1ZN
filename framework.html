Social Construction of Technology

What SCOT is:

The Social Construction of Technology (SCOT) theory posits that we define what technology is as a society, and in turn, technology defines society. People in favor of this viewpoint say that what a technology is for and how it is applied cannot be understood unless you understand how the technology is applied in the social context. The social context is what shapes the technology. (1)
